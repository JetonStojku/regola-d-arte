# Regola D'arte layout rules

- Keep mobile header to one compact row: logo, short booking CTA, menu button.
- Do not place primary nav, language choices, CTA, and social links in one undifferentiated row.
- Separate language switchers with spacing or a divider.
- Keep social/contact links visible on all pages through the shared shell.
- Avoid sparse text columns beside overloaded stacked cards.
- Prefer balanced content bands and even grids over nested cards inside cards.
- Use cards for repeated items, embeds, forms, and framed tools; do not make every section a card.
- Keep headings sized to their context; avoid hero-scale text inside compact panels.
- Check desktop and mobile widths before finishing layout work.
