# Materials content audit

## Confirmed facts from source materials

### Clinic identity

- Clinic name: `Regola D’arte`
- Brand framing: `“il vostro sorriso, la nostra arte”`
- The clinic positions itself as combining clinical competence with aesthetics, science with humanity.
- The clinic describes its philosophy around precision of intervention, material quality, attention to biomechanics, occlusion, function, and aesthetics.
- Treatments are described as customized to the patient’s needs.
- All treatment plans are preceded by an accurate diagnostic phase and developed with a multidisciplinary approach.

### Services explicitly listed in the Italian source

- Odontoiatria conservativa ed endodonzia
- Chirurgia orale e implantologia
- Implantologia a carico immediato
- Ortodonzia tradizionale e invisibile
- Trattamenti di estetica dentale
- Igiene orale professionali e programmi di prevenzione personalizzati

### Service-copy details that are explicitly present

- Conservative dentistry and endodontics focus on prevention and treatment of tooth lesions caused by caries or trauma.
- Implantology is described as replacing missing teeth with titanium artificial roots through osseointegration.
- Immediate-load implantology is described as placing temporary prosthesis within 36 hours and the final prosthesis after about 3 to 4 months.
- `All on Four` is mentioned as a technique used with immediate loading.
- Orthodontics includes both fixed braces and clear aligners.
- Dental aesthetics includes whitening, veneers, crowns, and cosmetic reconstruction concepts.
- Professional oral hygiene is described as plaque and tartar removal and is recommended 1 or 2 times per year depending on the patient and dentist advice.
